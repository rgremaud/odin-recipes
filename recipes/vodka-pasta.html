Vodka Pasta

Home

Description

Making a delicious vodka pasta from home is easy and quick. With a handful of ingrediants and a little magic you can enjoy this dish for dinner.

Ingrediants

  • One pound of pasta
  • Tomato Paste
  • One pound of Italian Sauage
  • One finely chopped onion
  • Three to four cloves of garlic
  • Vodka for deglazing pan
  • Heavy cream
  • Parm

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with water and add generous pinch of salt to the water
  2. Finally chop your onion and mince garlic
  3. Heat a couple tablespoons of olive oil in your pan on medium/high heat
  4. Add onions/garlic to pan and cook for several minutes
  5. Remove sausage from casings and add to pan, breaking up with wooden spoon. Cook until the meat is browned
  6. Add tomato paste to sausage meat and stir until well incoporated
  7. Cook pasta
  8. When pasta is cooked reserve one cup of pasta water and strain pasta
  9. Deglaze sausage with vodka
  10. Add pasta water to sausage and then mix in heavy cream
  11. Add pasta to the sauce and mix until well incoporated
  12. Add fresh shredded parm and serve immediately. Enjoy!
