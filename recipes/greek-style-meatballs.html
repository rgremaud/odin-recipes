Greek Style Meatballs

Home

Description

Time to fire up the grill! These greek style meatballs are sure to be a hit with the entire family.


Ingrediants

  • One pound of ground beef
  • One pound of ground lamb
  • One cup of panko bread crumbs
  • One egg
  • Handful of flat leaf parsley
  • One small onion finely chopped
  • Two cloves of garlic minced
  • Juice of half a lemon

Steps

  1. Finely chop your onion
  2. Mince garlic
  3. Finely chop your parsley
  4. Whisk your egg
  5. Mix all ingrediants by hand in a bowl with salt and pepper to taste
  6. Roll your meatballs
  7. Cook on grill
  8. Enjoy!
