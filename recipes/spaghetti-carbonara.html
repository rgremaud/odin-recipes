Spaghetti Carbonara

Home

Description

Make this classic Italian dish at home with an American spin on the pork products.


Ingrediants

  • 1 box of Spaghetti or Bucatini
  • 4 slices of bacon cut into cubes
  • 1 packet of panchetta cut into cubes (normally precut from store)
  • 4 gloves of garlic
  • Generous cup of shredded parmesan
  • 3 eggs

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with water and add generous amount of salt. Bring water to boil
  2. Slowly render your bacon and panchetta in a pan on medium/low
  3. Begin cooking pasta
  4. Once pork and panchetta are cooked add garlic to the pan and turn down to low
  5. Whisk eggs and add in parmesan along with heavy dose or black pepper
  6. Once pasta is al-dente reserve 1 cup of the cooking liquid and strain
  7. Slowly add pasta water to egg and cheese mixture, stiring gently
  8. Add egg mixture to pasta and pork. Mix well and serve immediately. Finish with more parmesan.
